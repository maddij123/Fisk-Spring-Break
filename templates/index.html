Fisk Students Spring Break

                

"Fisk Spring Break" refers to a period typically observed by Fisk University, a historically black university in Nashville, Tennessee, during the spring semester. It's a break from regular academic activities, allowing students to recharge, relax, and engage in various activities, including travel, community service, internships, or simply spending time with family and friends. Like other universities, Fisk University schedules its spring break at a specific time each year, usually in March or April, aligning with similar breaks in the academic calendar of other institutions across the United States. During this time, students may choose to participate in organized events or take personal time to rejuvenate before returning to their studies.
